Neatly rolled like a CINNAMON ROLL!


A cinnamon roll also known as cinnamon bun, cinnamon swirl, cinnamon Danish, and cinnamon snail.
It is a sweet roll commonly served in Northern Europe and North America.
A cinnamon roll consist of a rolled sheet of yeast-leavened dough onto which a cinnamon and sugar mixture is sprinkled over a thin coat of butter.
The dough is then rolled, cut into individual portions and baked.
